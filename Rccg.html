OUR VISSION/MISSION

To make Heaven.
To take as many people as possible with us.
To have a member of the Redeemed Christian Church of God in every family of all nations.
To accomplish No 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
To accomplish Nos. 2 & 3 above, we will plant churches within five
minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing
countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and
town of developed countries


We will pursue these objectives until every nation in the world is reached for JESUS CHRIST OUR LORD
