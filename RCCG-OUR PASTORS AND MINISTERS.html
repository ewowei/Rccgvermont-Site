THE REDEEMED CHRISTIAN CHURCH OF GOD

Meet: Pastor Victor Boardman


PASTOR VICTOR BOARDMAN WAS BORN AND RAISED IN AFRICA. AFTER GRADUATING FROM THE UNIVERSITY OF ILORIN HE IMMEDIATELY WENT TO WORK WITH THE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT (IN THE NATIONAL MUSEUM) AND BEGAN WORKING IN THE ENERGY INDUSTRY (AFRICAN PETROLEUM) SHORTLY THEREAFTER. WHILE WORKING IN THIS FIELD HE HAD AN ENCOUNTER WITH GOD AND CAME TO REALIZE THAT HE HAD A HIGHER CALLING. HE BECAME BORN AGAIN IN 1991. AFTER MARRYING HIS WIFE JOSEPHINE IN 1993 HE BECAME MORE ACTIVE WITHIN THEIR CHURCH AND WAS APPOINTED AS A MINISTER IN 1994. IN 1999 HE ATTENDED THE RCCG BIBLE COLLEGE AND RECEIVED A DIPLOMA IN THEOLOGY. IN 2000 HE RECEIVED HIS MASTERS DEGREE IN MARKETING FROM LASU. COMING TO AMERICA, HE SUCCEEDED IN GETTING HIS MASTERS OF SCIENCE IN LEADERSHIP FROM CHAMPLAIN COLLEGE, VERMONT. WORKING IN MENTAL HEALTH SECTION OF THE MAJOR HOSPITAL IN THE STATE, HE WENT FURTHER TO OBTAIN A CERTIFICATE IN SUBSTANCE ABUSE AS HE PREPARES FOR HIS LADC CERTIFICATION.

THE REDEEMED CHRISTIAN CHURCH OF GOD

Meet: Pastor Josephine Boardman


PASTOR JOSEPHINE BOARDMAN WAS BORN AND RAISED IN AFRICA. SHE HAS BEEN A MEMBER OF THE SCRIPTURE UNION SINCE HER TIME AT THE UNIVERSITY OF IFE. AFTER GRADUATION SHE WORKED AS A HIGH SCHOOL TEACHER BEFORE JOINING RANK XEROX AS AN ACCOUNTS MANAGER. SHE WAS ORDAINED AS A DEACONESS IN 1995 AND A PASTOR IN 2009. SHE MANAGES THE WOMEN'S GROUP AND THE SUNDAY SCHOOL DEPARTMENT OF THE LIVING WATER PARISH.

THE BOARDMAN FAMILY MOVED TO THE UNITED STATES IN 2005 AND CURRENTLY RESIDE IN SOUTH BURLINGTON, VERMONT.
