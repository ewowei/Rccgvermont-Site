OUR MINISTRIES

WOMEN MINISTRY

Women are women of the Word. We are committed to the increase of the body of Christ and tireless in their service. They support the vision in the house with their time, talent and resources.The mandate of the Women Leaders Support Group are to: Serve as Armor Bearers Support, Assist, and Run the Vision of the Wife of the Parish Pastor for the Women’s Ministry Acquire and Pass on Valuable Ethics for Effective Service in Ministry “Older women likewise are to be reverent in their behavior, not malicious gossips nor enslaved to much wine, teaching what is good, so that they may encourage the young women to love their husbands, to love their children, to be sensible, pure, workers at home, kind, being subject to their own husbands, so that the word of God will not be dishonored.” - Titus 2:3-5

YOUTH MINISTRY

“Don’t let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity”             - Timothy 4:12  It's all about having a personal relationship with almighty God. Faith is about action. Purity paves the way for godly intimacy. God's will is serving him and others everyday. God's word is alive and active, as I read and meditate on it.  Maximum freedom is found under God's authority. Truth is not relative; it is the person of Jesus Christ.

CHILDREN MINISTRY

we are passionate about bringing up godly children, the quality of material and standards of teaching our children, would help shape them in the present environment we live in. We are blessed to not only make the Children’s ministry fun but also educative. Also what and how we teach will shape the future of our children as Christians.
