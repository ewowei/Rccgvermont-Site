PURPOSE AND ORIGIN

THE REDEEMED CHRISTIAN CHURCH OF GOD

LIVING WATER PARISH, VT

Our Origin


The church was born when Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) North America was mandated to plant churches in the ‘ Virgin’ areas of the North East- a predominantly white area. Five churches including Vermont, Maine, New Hampshire and Rhode Island where birthday in 2005-2006 RCCG, Living Water Parish was inaugurated in May 2016 and God has increased the church from the humble beginning of the first service with only 5 people- The Pastor, his wife and three children at Holiday Inn, South Burlington Vermont before we moved to our present location We are trusting God to use the church to reach out to all the people of Vermont.

Our Purpose


To preach the gospel to every household in Vermont, draw men and women to the knowldeg that Jesus christ is the Way,the Truth and the Life. John 14:6.
