LIVING WATER PARISH, VT

OUR VALUE


We value the pursuit of God, thirst for His presence and to know more of Him through private and public worship, the Word and prayer. Psalm 42:1-2.

We want to minister by serving people the way He did with compassion, under the power and anointing of the Holy Spirit. Luke 14:16-20; John 20:21; Ephesians 5:2; Colossians 3:12

We value godly family life where parents live as examples and cultivate an environment in which the spiritual, emotional and social growth of their children can be fully accomplished. Deuteronomy 6:7; Proverbs 22:6; 2 Timothy 3:15
